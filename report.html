The 3-Phase Action Plan

This roadmap outlines the prioritized, step-by-step strategy for achieving SEO dominance. The plan is divided into three distinct phases, moving from fixing foundational issues to building local authority and finally establishing long-term content leadership.

The first phase is about fixing what's broken. We will address critical technical errors, resolve damaging inconsistencies, and optimize the website's core structure to create a solid base for future growth.

  • Technical SEO Remediation: Conduct a full audit with PageSpeed Insights and fix all Core Web Vitals issues (image optimization, code minification, etc.).
  • On-Page Optimization: Create new, dedicated landing pages for each key service (e.g., Acne Care, Hair Fall) with in-depth, keyword-rich content.
  • NAP & GBP Correction: Immediately correct the "Physiotherapist" listing on JustDial and fully optimize the Google Business Profile with services, photos, and a compelling description.

With a solid foundation, this phase focuses on building trust and visibility within the local Jabalpur market. We will expand the clinic's digital footprint and leverage its excellent reputation to attract more patients.

  • Local Citation Building: Systematically create consistent NAP listings on high-authority local and healthcare directories (Practo, HexaHealth, Sulekha).
  • Reputation Management: Implement a proactive strategy to solicit new reviews on Google and JustDial. Respond professionally and empathetically to all feedback to build trust.
  • Review Keyword Analysis: Analyze patient reviews for valuable keywords ("authentic Ayurvedic treatment," "best laser clinic") to inform future content strategy.

The final phase establishes the clinic as the go-to authority for dermatology in Jabalpur. By consistently creating valuable, educational content, we will drive sustained organic traffic and build a loyal community.

  • Content Marketing Engine: Develop a content calendar focused on the Ayurvedic/holistic approach. Create blog posts and articles answering common patient questions (e.g., "Ayurvedic remedies for hair fall").
  • Brand Building via Social Media: Launch and maintain active profiles on Facebook and Instagram. Share educational content, patient success stories, and behind-the-scenes glimpses to build community and E-A-T (Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness).
